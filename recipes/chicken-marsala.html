Chicken Marsala

Chicken Marsala is an Italian classic - praised for it's creamy marsala wine sauce. This dish is packed full of flavor from a blend of fresh herbs & spices. Best served over pasta, risotto, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, pounded about 1/4 inch thick
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flower
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter (divided)
  • 1 8-oz package pre-sliced bella mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons of finely chopped shallots (1 medium shallot should suffice)
  • 2 cloves of freshly chopped garlic
  • 2/3 cup chicken broth (use full sodium)
  • 2/3 cup dry marsala wine
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme
